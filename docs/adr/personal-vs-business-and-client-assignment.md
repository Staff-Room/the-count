# Capture personal-vs-business and client-assignment coding dimensions

- **Status:** Accepted
- **Date:** 2026-05-28
- **Canonical source:** https://www.notion.so/36e3ef6cafd581abb637f8ffd77ef888

## Decision

Add two coding dimensions beyond account/category: a **personal-vs-business** flag, and a
**client assignment** for revenue tracking.

## Context & Options

Sole-proprietor accounts mix personal and business activity, and revenue needs to be
attributable per client. These dimensions support clean separation and per-client revenue
reporting without a separate system.
